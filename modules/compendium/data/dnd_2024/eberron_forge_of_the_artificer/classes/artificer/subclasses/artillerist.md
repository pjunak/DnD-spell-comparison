# Artillerist

An Artillerist specializes in using magic to hurl energy, projectiles, and explosions on a battlefield.

### Eldritch Cannon
_3rd-level Artillerist feature_

Create Small/Tiny cannon (Magic action). Lasts 1 hr or until 0 HP.
**Activation:** Bonus Action to activate.
**Flamethrower:** 15ft cone, 2d8 Fire (Dex save half).
**Force Ballista:** 120ft range, Spell Attack, 2d8 Force + push 5 ft.
**Protector:** 10ft radius, 1d8 + Int mod Temp HP.

### Arcane Firearm
_5th-level Artillerist feature_

Carve sigils into rod/staff/wand/weapon. +1d8 damage to Artificer spells cast through it.

### Explosive Cannon
_9th-level Artillerist feature_

*   **Detonate:** Reaction when cannon takes damage: 3d10 Force to all within 20 ft (Dex save half). Destroys cannon.
*   **Firepower:** Cannon damage/Temp HP rolls increase by 1d8.

### Fortified Position
_15th-level Artillerist feature_

*   **Double Firepower:** Two cannons at once. Activate both with one Bonus Action.
*   **Shimmering Field:** Half Cover for allies within 10 ft of cannon.
