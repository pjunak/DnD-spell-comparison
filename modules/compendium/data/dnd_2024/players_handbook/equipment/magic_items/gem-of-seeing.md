*Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement)*

This gem has 3 charges. As a [Magic](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#MagicAction) action, you can expend 1 charge. For the next 10 minutes, you have [Truesight](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Truesight) out to 120 feet when you peer through the gem.
