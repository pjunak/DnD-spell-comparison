*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. While wearing them, your vision improves significantly out to a range of 1 foot, granting you [Darkvision](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Darkvision) within that range and Advantage on Intelligence ([Investigation](/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) checks made to examine something within that range.
