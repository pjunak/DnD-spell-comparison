This backpack has two side pouches and a central pouch.
**Capacity:** The central pouch can hold 8 cubic feet or 80 pounds of material. Each side pouch can hold 2 cubic feet or 20 pounds.
**Magic:** The haversack always weighs 5 pounds, regardless of its contents. Placing an object in the haversack follows the normal rules for interacting with objects. Retrieving an item from the haversack requires an action.
