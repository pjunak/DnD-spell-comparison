These pigments allow you to create three-dimensional objects by painting them in two dimensions. The paint flows from the pot to form the object you desire.
**Creation:** You can paint a door, a pit, or any other object. The object appears when you complete the painting.
**Volume:** The pot contains enough pigment to create 1,000 cubic feet of objects.
