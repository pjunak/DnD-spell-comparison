*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

The medallion has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge to cast [Detect Thoughts](/spells/2619099-detect-thoughts) (save DC 13) from it. The medallion regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn.
