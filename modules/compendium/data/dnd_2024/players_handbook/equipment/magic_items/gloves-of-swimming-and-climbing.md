*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing these gloves, you have a Climb Speed and a Swim Speed equal to your Speed, and you gain a +5 bonus to Strength ([Athletics](/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) checks made to climb or swim.
