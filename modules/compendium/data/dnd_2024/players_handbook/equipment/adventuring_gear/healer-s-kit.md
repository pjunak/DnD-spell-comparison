This kit is a leather pouch containing bandages, salves, and splints. The kit has 10 charges. As an action, you can expend one charge to restore 1 hit point to a creature that has 0 hit points, or you can expend one use to stabilize a creature that has 0 hit points, without needing to make a Wisdom (Medicine) check.
