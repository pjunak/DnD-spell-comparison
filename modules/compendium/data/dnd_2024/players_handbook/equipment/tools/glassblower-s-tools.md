The tools include a blowpipe, a small marver, blocks, and tweezers. You need a source of heat to work glass.

**Arcana, History:** Your knowledge of glassmaking grants you insight when you examine magical jars or glass objects.
**Investigation:** When you study a glass object, you can learn what it was used to contain.
