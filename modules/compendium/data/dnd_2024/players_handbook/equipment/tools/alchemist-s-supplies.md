This satchel contains glass beakers, metal frame stands, and common ingredients, such as chalk, salt, and powdered iron.

**Components:** Glass beakers, metal frame stands, bronze mortar and pestle, common alchemical ingredients including salt, powdered iron, and purified water.
**Arcana:** Proficiency with these supplies allows you to unlock more information on Arcana checks involving potions and similar fluids.
**Investigation:** When you inspect an area for clues, proficiency with these supplies grants additional insight into any chemicals or other substances that might have been used in the area.
