# Serpent Venom

*Poison (Injury Poison)*

**Cost:** 200 GP

A creature subjected to Serpent Venom must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) Poison damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.
