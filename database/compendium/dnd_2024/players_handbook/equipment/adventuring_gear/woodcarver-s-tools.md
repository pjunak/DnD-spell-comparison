This kit includes a knife, a gouge, and a small saw.

**Arcana, History:** Your expertise aids you in identifying the nature of magical wooden objects and the history of their creation.
**Nature:** Your knowledge of wood grants you advantage on checks to identify trees and wooden objects.
**Repair:** As part of a short rest, you can repair a single damaged wooden object.
