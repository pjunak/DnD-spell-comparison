# Wyvern Poison

*Poison (Injury Poison)*

**Cost:** 1200 GP

A creature subjected to Wyvern Poison makes a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (7d6) Poison damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.
