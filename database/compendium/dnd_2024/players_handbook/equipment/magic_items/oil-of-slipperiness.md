*Potion, Uncommon*

One vial of this oil can cover one Medium or smaller creature, along with the equipment itâ€™s wearing and carrying (one additional vial is required for each size category above Medium). Applying the oil takes 10 minutes. The affected creature then gains the effect of the [Freedom of Movement](/spells/2618921-freedom-of-movement) spell for 8 hours.

Alternatively, the oil can be poured on the ground as a [Magic](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#MagicAction) action, where it covers a 10-foot square, duplicating the effect of the [Grease](/spells/2618957-grease) spell in that area for 8 hours.

This sticky, black unguent is thick and heavy, but it flows quickly when poured.
