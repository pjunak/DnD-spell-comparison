*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing this pendant, you gain the following benefits.

***Life Preservation.*** Whenever you make a [Death Saving Throw](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DeathSavingThrow), you can change a roll of 9 or lower to a 10, turning a failed save into a successful one.

***Natural Healing Boost.*** Whenever you roll a Hit Point Die to regain Hit Points, double the number of Hit Points it restores.
