Requires attunement. This magic weapon warns you of danger.
**Advantage:** You have advantage on initiative rolls.
**Awareness:** You and any of your companions within 30 feet of you can't be surprised, except when incapacitated by something other than nonmagical sleep.
**Awakening:** The weapon magically awakens you and your companions within range if any of you are sleeping naturally when combat begins.
