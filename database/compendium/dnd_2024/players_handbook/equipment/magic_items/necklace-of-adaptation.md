*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing this necklace, you can breathe normally in any environment, and you have Advantage on saving throws made to avoid or end the [Poisoned](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#PoisonedCondition) condition.
