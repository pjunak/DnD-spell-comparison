This item is a large, enclosed iron barrel with a hidden catch. It can hold two Medium creatures.
**Movement:** It has a walking speed of 30 feet and a swimming speed of 30 feet.
**Armor Class:** 20
**Hit Points:** 200
**Immunities:** Poison, Psychic.
The apparatus allows you to breathe underwater and provides standard cover. It has 10 levers that control its movement and attacks (Claw inputs).
