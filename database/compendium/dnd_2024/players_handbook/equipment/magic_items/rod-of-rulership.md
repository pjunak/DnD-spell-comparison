*Rod, Rare (Requires Attunement)*

You can take a [Magic](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#MagicAction) action to present the rod and command obedience from each creature of your choice that you can see within 120 feet of yourself. Each target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or have the [Charmed](/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#CharmedCondition) condition for 8 hours. While Charmed in this way, the creature regards you as its trusted leader.
