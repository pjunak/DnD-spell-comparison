**Speed:** The speed of a vehicle is how far it travels in 1 hour (or 1 minute for an airship).
**Crew:** A vehicle needs a crew to operate smoothly. If the vehicle is short of crew, its speed is halved.
**Passengers:** The number of passengers the vehicle can carry in addition to the crew.
**Cargo:** The amount of cargo the vehicle can carry in tons (1 ton = 2,000 pounds).
**AC and HP:** A vehicle's Armor Class and Hit Points. If a vehicle drops to 0 Hit Points, it is destroyed.
