This kit includes a knife, a small mallet, an edger, a hole punch, thread, and leather scraps.

**Arcana:** Your expertise in working with leather grants you insight when you examine magical items that are crafted from leather.
**Investigation:** You gain additional insight when studying leather items or clues related to them, as you draw on your knowledge of leather to pick out details that others would overlook.
