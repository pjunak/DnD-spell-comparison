This kit contains ink, a dozen sheets of parchment, and three quills.

**Components:** Ink, a dozen sheets of parchment, three quills.
**Arcana:** Although calligraphy is of little help in deciphering the content of magical writings, proficiency with these supplies aids in identifying who wrote a script of a magical nature.
**History:** This tool proficiency grants you advantage on a History check to analyze or investigate any text, verifying its authenticity or identifying its original author.
