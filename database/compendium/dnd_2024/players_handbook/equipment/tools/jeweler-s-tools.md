These tools include a small saw and hammer, files, pliers, and tweezers.

**Arcana:** Proficiency with jeweler's tools grants you knowledge about the reputed mystical uses of gems. This insight ensures you identify gems and restore them to their best condition.
**Investigation:** When you inspect jeweled objects, your proficiency with jeweler's tools grants you clues as to the original owner or the purpose of a particular piece of jewelry.
