This kit includes hammers, tongs, charcoal, rags, and a whetstone.

**History:** Your expertise aids you in identifying metal objects, including when they were created and their likely place or culture of origin.
**Investigation:** When you inspect a metal object, you can gain insight into how it was made and what it was used for.
**Repair:** With access to your tools and an open flame hot enough to make metal pliable, you can restore 10 hit points to a damaged metal object for each hour of work.
