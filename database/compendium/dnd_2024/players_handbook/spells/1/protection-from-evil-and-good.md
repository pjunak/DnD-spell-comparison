*Level 1 Abjuration (Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Warlock, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S, M (a flask of Holy Water worth 25+ GP, which the spell consumes)

**Duration:** Concentration up to 10 minutes

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is protected against creatures that are Aberrations, Celestials, Elementals, Fey, Fiends, or Undead. The protection grants several benefits.
