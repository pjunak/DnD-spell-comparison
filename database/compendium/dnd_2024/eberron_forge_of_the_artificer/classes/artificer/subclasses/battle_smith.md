# Battle Smith

Armies require protection, and someone has to put them back together if defenses fail. A combination of protector and medic, a Battle Smith is an expert at defending others and repairing both material and personnel.

### Battle Ready
_3rd-level Battle Smith feature_

*   **Arcane Empowerment:** Use Int modifier for attacks/damage with magic weapons.
*   **Weapon Knowledge:** Proficiency with Martial weapons.

### Steel Defender
_3rd-level Battle Smith feature_

Create a Steel Defender companion. Acts on your turn (Dodge unless commanded).
**Command:** Bonus Action.
**Revive:** Magic Action + spell slot (1 min). Create new on Long Rest.

### Extra Attack
_5th-level Battle Smith feature_

Attack twice. Can forgo one attack to command Defender.

### Arcane Jolt
_9th-level Battle Smith feature_

When you or Defender hit with magic weapon/attack:
*   **Destructive:** Extra 2d6 Force damage.
*   **Restorative:** Heal 2d6 HP to creature within 30 ft.
Uses = Int mod. Once per turn.

### Improved Defender
_15th-level Battle Smith feature_

*   **Improved Jolt:** Damage/Healing increases to 4d6.
*   **Improved Deflection:** Defender deals 1d4 + Int mod Force damage when using Deflect Attack.
