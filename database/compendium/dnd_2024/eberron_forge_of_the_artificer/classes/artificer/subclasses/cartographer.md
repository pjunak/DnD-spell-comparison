# Cartographer

A Cartographer is a master of exploration, using magical maps to guide allies and traverse space.

## Subclass Features

### Tools of the Trade
_3rd-level Cartographer feature_

*   **Tool Proficiency:** Calligrapher’s Supplies and Cartographer’s Tools.
*   **Scroll Crafting:** Scribing Spell Scroll time halved.

### Cartographer Spells
_3rd-level Cartographer feature_

| Artificer Level | Spells |
|:---:|:---|
| 3rd | *faerie fire*, *guiding bolt*, *healing word* |
| 5th | *locate object*, *mind spike* |
| 9th | *call lightning*, *clairvoyance* |
| 13th | *banishment*, *locate creature* |
| 17th | *scrying*, *teleportation circle* |

### Adventurer's Atlas
_3rd-level Cartographer feature_

Create magic maps for creatures (1 + Int mod).
*   **Awareness:** +1d4 to Initiative.
*   **Positioning:** Know location of other map holders. Can target other holders with spells regardless of sight/cover.

### Mapping Magic
_3rd-level Cartographer feature_

*   **Illuminated Cartography:** Cast *faerie fire* without slot. Uses = Int mod.
*   **Portal Jump:** Spend half move to teleport 10 ft or near map holder.

### Guided Precision
_5th-level Cartographer feature_

Add Int mod to damage of Cartographer spell or attack vs *faerie fire* target.
Concentration on *faerie fire* can't be broken by damage.

### Ingenious Movement
_9th-level Cartographer feature_

When using Flash of Genius, target can teleport 30 ft.

### Superior Atlas
_15th-level Cartographer feature_

*   **Safe Haven:** Map holder at 0 HP can destroy map to heal 2x Artificer level and teleport to you.
*   **Unerring Path:** Cast *find the path* without slot.
