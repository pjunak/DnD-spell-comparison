# Armorer

An artificer who specializes as an Armorer modifies armor to function almost like a second skin. The armor is enhanced to hone the artificer’s magic, unleash potent attacks, and generate a formidable defense.

## Subclass Features

### Tools of the Trade
_3rd-level Armorer feature_

*   **Armor Training:** Proficiency with Heavy armor.
*   **Tool Proficiency:** Proficiency with Smith’s Tools.
*   **Armor Crafting:** Armor crafting time halved.

### Armorer Spells
_3rd-level Armorer feature_

| Artificer Level | Spells |
|:---:|:---|
| 3rd | *magic missile*, *thunderwave* |
| 5th | *mirror image*, *shatter* |
| 9th | *hypnotic pattern*, *lightning bolt* |
| 13th | *fire shield*, *greater invisibility* |
| 17th | *passwall*, *wall of force* |

### Arcane Armor
_3rd-level Armorer feature_

Turn worn armor into Arcane Armor (Magic action w/ Smith's Tools).
*   **No Strength Requirement.**
*   **Quick Don/Doff:** Utilize action.
*   **Spellcasting Focus:** Can use armor as focus.

### Armor Model
_3rd-level Armorer feature_

Choose model: Dreadnaught, Guardian, or Infiltrator. Can change on Short/Long Rest. Add Int mod to attack/damage with model weapon.

**Dreadnaught:**
*   **Force Demolisher:** Simple Melee (Reach), 1d10 Force. Hit smaller creature: Push/Pull 10 ft.
*   **Giant Stature:** BA, 1 min. Reach +5 ft, Size becomes Large. Uses = Int mod.

**Guardian:**
*   **Thunder Pulse:** Simple Melee, 1d8 Thunder. Hit: Disadvantage on attacks vs others.
*   **Defensive Field:** BA while Bloodied, gain Temp HP = Artificer level.

**Infiltrator:**
*   **Lightning Launcher:** Simple Ranged (90/300), 1d6 Lightning. Once/turn extra 1d6.
*   **Powered Steps:** Speed +5 ft.
*   **Dampening Field:** Advantage on Stealth.

### Extra Attack
_5th-level Armorer feature_

Attack twice when taking Attack action.

### Improved Armorer
_9th-level Armorer feature_

*   **Armor Replication:** Learn extra infusion plan (Armor). Create extra item (Armor).
*   **Improved Arsenal:** +1 bonus to attack/damage with Arcane Armor weapon.

### Perfected Armor
_15th-level Armorer feature_

**Dreadnaught:** Demolisher 2d6. Giant Stature: Reach +10 ft, Huge size, Adv on Str checks/saves.
**Guardian:** Pulse 1d10. Reaction when creature ends turn within 30 ft: Pull 25 ft, melee attack if close. Uses = Int mod.
**Infiltrator:** Launcher 2d6. Hit target glimmers (Dim Light, Disadv on attacks vs you). BA: Fly speed = 2x Speed until end of turn. Uses = Int mod.
