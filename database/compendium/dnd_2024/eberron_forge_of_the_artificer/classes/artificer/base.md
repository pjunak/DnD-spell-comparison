### Spellcasting
_1st-level Artificer feature_

You have learned how to channel magical energy through objects.
**Tools Required:** You produce your Artificer spells through tools. You must have a spellcasting focus (Thieves' tools, Tinker's tools, or artisan's tools) in hand when you cast any spell with this class.
**Cantrips:** You start with two artificer cantrips. You learn additional cantrips at higher levels.
**Prepared Spells:** You prepare a list of artificer spells equal to your Intelligence modifier + half your Artificer level (rounded down, minimum of one spell).

### Tinker's Magic
_1st-level Artificer feature_

You know the *mending* cantrip.
As a Magic action while holding Tinker’s Tools, you can create one item in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of yourself (e.g., a tiny mechanical object, a light source). See the Player’s Handbook for full list. The item lasts until you finish a Long Rest. Uses = Int mod (min 1). Regain on Long Rest.

### Replicate Magic Item
_2nd-level Artificer feature_

You learn to replicate magic items.
**Plans Known:** Choose 4 plans (e.g., Bag of Holding, Sending Stones). Replace one on level up.
**Creating an Item:** At end of Long Rest, create one or two magic items using Tinker's Tools.
**Limit:** Magic Items column shows max active items. Oldest vanishes if exceeded.
**Duration:** Items vanish 1d4 days after you die.

### Artificer Subclass
_3rd-level Artificer feature_

Choose a subclass: Alchemist, Armorer, Artillerist, Battle Smith, or Cartographer.

### Ability Score Improvement
_4th-level Artificer feature_

Gain the Ability Score Improvement feat or another feat of your choice. Repeat at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Magic Item Tinker
_6th-level Artificer feature_

Options for Replicate Magic Item:
*   **Charge Magic Item:** BA, touch own item, expend spell slot to recharge charges equal to slot level.
*   **Drain Magic Item:** BA, touch own item, destroy it to regain spell slot (L1 for Common, L2 for Uncommon/Rare). Once per Long Rest.
*   **Transmute Magic Item:** Magic action, touch own item, transform into another plan you know. Once per Long Rest.

### Flash of Genius
_7th-level Artificer feature_

Reaction when you or creature within 30 ft fails check/save: add Int mod to roll. Uses = Int mod. Regain on Long Rest.

### Magic Item Adept
_10th-level Artificer feature_

Attune to up to 4 magic items.

### Spell-Storing Item
_11th-level Artificer feature_

End of Long Rest, store L1-L2 spell (action casting time) in weapon/focus. Creature holding it can use Magic action to cast spell using your Int. Uses = 2 * Int mod.

### Advanced Artifice
_14th-level Artificer feature_

*   **Magic Item Savant:** Attune to up to 5 magic items.
*   **Refreshed Genius:** Regain one use of Flash of Genius on Short Rest.

### Magic Item Master
_18th-level Artificer feature_

Attune to up to 6 magic items.

### Epic Boon
_19th-level Artificer feature_

Gain an Epic Boon feat.

### Soul of Artifice
_20th-level Artificer feature_

*   **Cheat Death:** If drop to 0 HP, disintegrate one of your magic items (Uncommon/Rare) to regain 20 * (items destroyed) HP.
*   **Magical Guidance:** Regain all Flash of Genius uses on Short Rest if attuned to at least one item.
